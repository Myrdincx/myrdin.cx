## General Information

Lainring is a user-created, unofficial decentralized webring associated with the community of [Lainchan](https://lainchan.org/), an anonymous imageboard. To become a member, just visit the most recent webring thread and share your website along with a banner sized `240x60`.

Please note that being part of the webring does not mean you endorse or support any of the other websites included in it.
